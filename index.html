AI for Mental Health Awareness (AI4A Lab Project)

Mathematical Analysis of Parthenium Weed Spread (Calculus-Based Study)

I am working on a calculus-based project to study the spread of the Parthenium plant, an invasive species that affects agricultural land and ecosystems. The project focuses on understanding how the plant spreads across regions and identifying factors that influence its growth. By applying concepts of limits and rate of change, the aim is to model the growth pattern and predict the spread over time. This study helps in understanding environmental impact and can contribute to better control and prevention strategies.
